About Sinapi Group

Sinapi Group is dedicated to preserving the rich heritage of the Zulu people through the art of shoemaking. With a deep respect for traditional craftsmanship, we design and manufacture authentic Zulu shoes that embrace cultural significance while offering modern functionality.

Our journey began with a passion for African artistry and a commitment to creating footwear that reflects history, identity, and excellence. Whether you are looking for footwear for everyday wear or special cultural events, Sinapi Group ensures that every pair is made with quality materials and attention to detail.

Join us in keeping tradition alive, one step at a time.
